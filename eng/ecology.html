Token ecosystem

Util ity

Token is only used as a means of payment for the services provided by SDEP platform and as reward to users, and will not be used in the pricing of products or settlement of transactions

Reward mechanism

Any third party that provides computing power support to multi-source data exchange with be rewarded with token

Incentive mechanism

Prime data providers will be rewarded with token when they connect to the system

Fees

Data sellers have to pay token to the platform as transaction fees
