Pain points

Oligopoly & monopoly

Internet giants dominate nearly half of the data worldwide and have the “God’s Eye”. How to disrupt this data monopoly?

Information silo

SMEs haven’t translated their data into assets and suffer low data flow speed, which makes them information islands.

Gray deal

Transactions that are not primarily facilitated by dealmakers, and are mostly grey deals

Lack of trust

Data security cannot be guaranteed in the data exchange process, due to a lack of trust
